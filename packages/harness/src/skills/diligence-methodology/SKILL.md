# Diligence Methodology

## Phase Sequencing (Critical)

1. **Independent public view FIRST**: Never look at private materials before forming your own view from public sources. This prevents anchoring bias to management's narrative.

2. **Private data room review**: With your independent view established, now review the VDR. Actively look for confirmations AND contradictions of your public view.

3. **Financial deep-dive**: Load KPIs and build the revenue bridge. Cross-reference against the CIM and management presentation. The numbers should reconcile — if they don't, that's a finding.

4. **Cross-source reconciliation**: For every material claim management makes, find the supporting (or contradicting) evidence. Document the delta.

5. **Issue creation**: Every material finding becomes a tracked issue with severity, workstream assignment, and recommended next action.

6. **Memo drafting**: Write each section as findings accumulate. Don't wait until the end. The memo is a living document during diligence.

## Evidence Standards
- Every claim must cite at least one evidence source
- Provenance must be labeled: public_live (web sources) vs. synthetic_private (VDR/finance)
- Confidence scoring: High (0.85+) = multiple corroborating sources; Medium (0.70-0.84) = single reliable source; Low (<0.70) = inferred or partial data
- Contradictions between public and private sources are always flagged as issues
