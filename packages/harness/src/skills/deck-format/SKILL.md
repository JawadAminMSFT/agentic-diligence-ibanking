# IC Summary Deck Format

When generating a summary presentation deck using artifacts-generate_deck, structure the content as a series of slides that tell the investment story concisely. The deck should be suitable for a 15-minute Investment Committee presentation.

## Slide Structure

### Slide 1: Title
- Deal code name and sector
- Date and analyst team
- Confidentiality notice

### Slide 2: Executive Summary
- One-line thesis
- Key metrics: ARR, growth rate, NRR, GRR
- Preliminary verdict with confidence level

### Slide 3: Company Overview
- Business model description
- Target market and TAM
- Product positioning and competitive landscape
- Customer count and segment mix

### Slide 4: Commercial Assessment
- Customer concentration analysis (top 5/10 %)
- Segment-level metrics table
- Public vs. private narrative comparison
- Key commercial risks flagged

### Slide 5: Financial Deep-Dive
- Revenue bridge waterfall (new, expansion, contraction, churn)
- NRR/GRR by segment
- Gross margin and unit economics
- Cash position and runway

### Slide 6: Retention & Cohorts
- Cohort retention curves by vintage
- Segment-level GRR trend
- Logo churn vs. revenue churn distinction
- Deteriorating cohort callouts

### Slide 7: Legal & Contractual Risks
- Change-of-control exposure (% ARR at risk)
- IP ownership status
- Key contract provisions summary
- Regulatory compliance gaps

### Slide 8: Key Issues Summary
- Table of all flagged issues: title, severity, workstream
- Sorted by severity descending
- Recommended next actions per issue

### Slide 9: Recommendation
- Verdict: Proceed / Proceed with conditions / Pass
- Conditions to resolve
- Valuation implications
- Proposed next steps (management meetings, expert calls, data requests)

### Slide 10: Appendix — Data Sources
- List of all evidence sources cited (VDR documents, public sources)
- Provenance labels and confidence scores

## Formatting Rules
- Each slide should have a clear title and 3-5 bullet points maximum
- Use specific numbers, not qualitative language: "$62.4M ARR" not "strong revenue"
- Bold key metrics and risk callouts
- Include source references: "(vdr-001)", "(wr-003)"
- Keep total slide count between 8-12
- Each slide's content should be self-contained and readable independently
